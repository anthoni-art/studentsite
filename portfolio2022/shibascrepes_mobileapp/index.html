Shiba's Crepes Mobile App

A modern-day food service app, catering to modern-day society.

A Japanese crepe shop with integrated technology services. In this project, I was assigned to create a mobile app design for my interface design Ⅱ class. For the design process I created two style tiles, a sitemap, and a wireframe. I then gathered graphic assets and started out with a first draft design. After the first draft, I went back and revised it with the changes to make the design feel more modern.

Service: App Design
Software Adobe XD, Photoshop, Illustrator
Skills: UX, UI
