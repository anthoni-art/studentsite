Book Covers

Book Trilogy

This project was from my ART125 Intro to Graphic Design class and it was to create 3 book covers for a trilogy. I chose stories by David Sedaris, an author who loves to share some of his life stories with a hint of humor. The process of making our book covers took many sketches, and then creating three different styles of one story. After choosing which style was best we worked on the other two stories in the style we chose.

Client: ART125 Intro to Graphic Design
Service: Book Cover Design
Skills: Adobe Illustrator Experience
