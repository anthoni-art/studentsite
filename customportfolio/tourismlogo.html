Book Covers

Book Trilogy

This project was from my Intro to Graphic Design class and it was to create 3 book covers for a trilogy. I chose stories by David Sedaris, an author who loves to share some of his life stories with a hint of humor. The process of making our book covers took many sketches, and then creating three different styles of one story. After choosing which style was best we worked on the other two stories in the style we chose.

Client: HIRO Systems & Solutions
Service: Web Design
Skills: Adobe Illustrator Skills
