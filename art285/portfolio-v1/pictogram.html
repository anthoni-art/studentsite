Olympic Pictogram

Designing our own Olympic Pictogram

This project was for my graphic symbolism class. We chose 4 olympic sports and designed pictogams for each one. The process took countless sketching and revising in order to perfect our designs. I used illustrator to create the designs and photoshop for the mockups.

Client: Olympics
Service: Sketches and Digital Illustration
Skills: Adobe Illustrator Experience
